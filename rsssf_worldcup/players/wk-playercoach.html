The champions:

Jackie Charlton (1966), Didi (1958/1962), Dunga (1994), Giovanni Ferrari
(1934/1938), Alfredo Foni (1938), Jürgen Klinsmann (1990), Ricardo
Lavolpe (1978), Diego Maradona (1986), Daniel Passarella (1978/1986),
Rudi Völler (1990) and Berti Vogts (1974) won World Cups as players.

Alf Ramsey (1966) and Lionel Scaloni (2022) won the World Cup as coaches.

Franz Beckenbauer (1974 as player and 1990 as coach), Mario Zagallo
(1958/1962 as player and 1970 as coach) and Didier Deschamps (1998 as player
and 2018 as coach) are the only who won World Cups in both functions.

Closest and farest:

Raul Cárdenas (1962-1970), Didi (1962-1970), Jürgen Klinsmann (1998-2006),
Niko Kovac (2006-2014), Henri Michel (1978-1986), Rudi Vöeller (1994-2002)
and Mario Zagallo (1962-1970) were in World Cup as coaches only 8 years after
as player.

Tim (1938-1982) were in World Cup as coach 44 years after as player.
