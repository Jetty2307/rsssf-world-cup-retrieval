Rio de Janeiro, July 16, 1950.

         Pérez, Miguez, Schiaffino, Morán.
Brazil: Barbosa, Augusto, Juvenal, Bauer, Danilo, Bigode, Friaça, Zizinho,
        Ademir, Jair, Chico.
