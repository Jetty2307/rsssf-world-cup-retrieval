England, 11-30 July 1966 (Birmingham, Liverpool, London, Manchester, Middlesborough, Sheffield, Sunderland). Decided at FIFA Congress in Rome, 22 Aug 1960. Entries deadline 15 Dec 1963. 74 teams entered (6 for the first time), 53 actually played (SAF banned for apartheid politics). Qualifications drawn 31 Jan 1964 in Zürich. Finals drawn 6 Jan 1966 in London.
