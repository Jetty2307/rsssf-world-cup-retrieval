Sweden, 6 - 29 June 1958 (Boraas, Eskilstuna, Gothenburg, Halmstad, Hälsingborg, Malmö, Nörrkoping, Orebro, Sandviken, Solna, Uddevalla, Västeräs). Decided at FIFA Congress in Rio de Janeiro, 22 Jul 1950. Entries deadline 31 Mar 1956. 55 teams entered, 48 actually played (11 for the first time). Qualifications drawn 27 Apr 1956 in Lausanne (playoffs 15 Dec 1957 in Zürich). Finals drawn 8 Feb 1958 in Stockholm

General Note: The placement in groups was determined solely on the base of points, goal difference was disregarded. This led to playoff matches in three groups (curiosly, teams with worse g/d won, in one case it was equal).
