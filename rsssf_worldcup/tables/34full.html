Italy, 27 May to 10 Jun 1934 (Bologna, Genova, Firenze, Roma, Milano, Napoli, Torino, Trieste) Decided at 8 Oct 1932. Entries deadline 28 Feb 1933. 32 countries entered (21 for the first time), 32 seeded, 29 played (CHI, PER, TUR withdrew). Qualifications drawn 22 May 1933 at Paris. Finals drawn 3 May 1934 at Rome.
