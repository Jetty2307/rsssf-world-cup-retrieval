Oceanian Nations Cup 2008


Match Details


The top three teams from the 2007 South Pacific Games (New Caledonia, Fiji, and Vanuatu) 
joined New Zealand in a four-team pool.

The 2008 OFC Nations Cup doubles as Oceania's 2010 World Cup qualifying
section: the top team will play a home and away matches with an Asia's 5th 
place nation for a place in the World Cup finals.
