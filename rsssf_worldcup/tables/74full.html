Germany, 13 Jun to 7 Jul 1974 (West Berlin, Düsseldorf, Dortmund, Frankfurt, Gelsenkirchen, Hamburg, Hannover, München, Stuttgart). Decided at FIFA Congress in London at 6 Jul 1966. Entries deadline 30 Jun 1971. 99 teams entered, 92 actually played. Qualifications drawn 17 Jul 1971 at Düsseldorf. Finals drawn 5 Jan 1974 at Frankfurt.
