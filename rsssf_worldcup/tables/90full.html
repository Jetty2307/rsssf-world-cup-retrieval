Italy, 8 June - 8 July 1990 (Bari, Bologna, Cagliari, Florentia, Genoa, Milano, Napoli, Palermo, Roma, Torino, Udine, Verona). Decided by FIFA Executive Commitee at Zürich, 19 May 1984. Entries deadline 30 Sep 1987. 116 teams entered, 105 actually played (3 for the first time; MEX expulsed; including LBA withdrawn during group stage). Qualifications draw 12 Dec 1987 in Zürich. Finals drawn 9 Dec 1989 in Roma.
